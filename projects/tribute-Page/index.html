David Lekuta Rudisha

David Rudisha is a Kenyan athlete who runs the 800 meters. He was born in 17 December 1988. He is the 2012 and 2016 Olympic champion, World champion and world record holder in the 800 metres. He has won a record 3 consecutive Track and Field Athlete of the Year awards (tied with Carl Lewis), and also won the IAAF World Athlete of the Year award in 2010.

Rudisha went to Kimuron Secondary School in Iten, Keiyo District, which is known for nurturing several top runners. Initially he was a 400 metres runner, but his coach, Colm O'Connell, prompted him to try 800 m. In 2006 he became the world junior champion over that distance.
